PRO TIP

The Secret to Efficient Mining

Alex Digger

Mining Expert

3 min read | March 10, 2025

Branch mining at Y-level 12 is the most efficient way to find diamonds. Create a main tunnel and branch out every 3 blocks to maximize your chances of finding rare ores while minimizing the blocks you need to mine.

Why Branch Mining Works

Branch mining allows you to expose the maximum number of blocks with the minimum amount of effort. By creating branches that are spaced 3 blocks apart, you ensure that you don't miss any potential ore veins while avoiding redundant mining.

Pro Tip

Always carry a water bucket while mining to counter unexpected lava pockets. Water can quickly solidify lava into obsidian, preventing damage to yourself and valuable items.

Essential Equipment

  • Iron/Diamond Pickaxe: Iron is sufficient, but diamond is more efficient
  • Torches: At least two stacks to properly light your tunnels
  • Water Bucket: Essential for dealing with lava
  • Food: Steaks or golden carrots are recommended
  • Spare Tools: Bring backups for long mining sessions

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Dig down to Y-level 12 (check with F3 on Java Edition)
  2. Create a main tunnel in any direction
  3. Every 3 blocks, dig a side tunnel perpendicular to your main tunnel
  4. Continue each branch tunnel for about 20-30 blocks
  5. Place torches every 8-10 blocks to prevent mob spawning
  6. Mark explored tunnels with a torch or sign at the entrance
Ore Distribution by Y-Level
  • Diamond: Y-levels 5-16 (most common at Y=11)
  • Gold: Y-levels 5-29
  • Redstone: Y-levels 5-12
  • Lapis Lazuli: Y-levels 13-18
  • Iron: Found throughout, but common between Y=24 and Y=54
