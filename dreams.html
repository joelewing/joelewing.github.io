Deny's

May 4, 2021

I find myself in an alternate universe. Everything seems to be the same as my normal universe ... and then I realize that Denny's doesn't exist! Instead there's a restaraunt called Deny's, which is pronounced like the name Denise. People think I'm crazy for believing that a restaurant called Denny's exists, and I spend the rest of the dream wondering what happened to Denny's.
