Ramblings of a madman

These silly winds we relentlessly pursue

these old things being called new

we were meant to believe that this falsehood was true.

But the smoke floats by

not stopping, yet singing a harsh lullaby

whispering old words so that we may once again try.

The wheels of time turn

while heaven seems contented to watch the world burn

watching us forget what we were meant to learn

Remember the fire from paradise that was found

Anointing humanity forever on holy ground

How beautiful the chains of nature’s subjection became unbound.

For mankind learned to create

Forever would imagination inundate

Guiding humanity’s quest to reach a higher state.

Now these old notions have been undone

Things meant to be serious are now just silly fun

Tell me, is there laughing when the world's end has begun.

Convince me, where is the power

Of entertainment every minute of every hour

While the cove may be sweet the sea has turned sour.

Once again must we pick up tools, not foolish toys

Act like grown-up men, not childish boys

Listen not to the wind, heed not the noise.
